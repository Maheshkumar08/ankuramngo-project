About Ankurm Foundation

Empowering children with special needs through education, therapy, and love.

How Did Ankurm Foundation Start?

Ankurm Foundation was born out of a heartfelt realization that children with special needs were underserved in terms of quality education and therapies. Our founder felt compelled to create a safe and inclusive space where every child could receive the care, education, and therapies they deserve to thrive.

What Inspired Us?

We were inspired by the belief that every child, no matter their challenges, holds immense potential waiting to be unlocked. Our mission is to focus on possibilities, not limitations, and help children grow with confidence and independence.

What Makes Us Unique?

At Ankurm Foundation, we provide individualized attention and personalized therapy programs. Our approach combines emotional care, academic learning, and inclusive education, where children with and without special needs learn together in harmony.

The Meaning Behind “Ankurm”

“Ankurm” is derived from the Hindi word “Ankur”, meaning a sprout — a symbol of new beginnings, hope, and potential. Each child at Ankurm is nurtured like a sprout, growing into a confident, capable individual.
